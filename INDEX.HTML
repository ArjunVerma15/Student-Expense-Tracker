Student Expense Tracker

Welcome to the Student Expense Tracker

This web application is designed to help you, a student, take control of your finances. It provides tools to track your expenses, set a budget, and plan for your financial goals.

Key Features

  • Expense Tracking: Categorize and record your daily expenses.
  • Budget Management: Set a monthly budget and monitor your spending.
  • Savings Goals: Define and track your progress towards financial goals with target dates.
  • Profile Management: Personalize your experience by setting your name, currency, and savings goals.
  • Visualizations: View your expense patterns with an interactive chart.
  • Saving Tips: Get helpful tips to improve your saving habits.

How to Use the App

1. Manage Your Profile

Personalize your profile by adding your name, setting your default currency, and specifying your savings goal.

2. Set Savings Goals

Define your financial goals, set a target date, and track your progress in the "Savings Goals" section.

3. Set Your Budget

Start by setting your monthly budget in the "Budget" section. This will help you track how your expenses compare to your income.

4. Track Your Expenses

Record your daily expenses in the "Expenses" section. Be sure to categorize each expense for better tracking.

Saving Tips

Tip 1: Create a budget and track your spending to see where your money is going.

Tip 2: Reduce unnecessary expenses, such as eating out or buying coffee daily.

Tip 3: Look for student discounts on transportation, entertainment, and textbooks.

Tip 4: Cook meals at home instead of eating out to save money on food.

Tip 5: Use free resources like the library and online courses to minimize expenses.

Tip 6: Start saving early, even small amounts can add up over time.

Tip 7: Avoid taking on unnecessary debt, and pay off student loans as quickly as possible.

Tip 8: Consider part-time work or internships to supplement your income.
